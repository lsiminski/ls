Movie Poster Redesign

Date:October 2013

Type:typography

A typographical redesign of the Bridesmaids movie poster.

I found this project to be a good opportunity to experiment with photography and paper-cuts. I created a representation of the beautiful, floral paper-cuts you can find in wedding invitations, and focused on the humorous fighting throughout the movie in the way I photographed my models displaying it.
